Privacy Policy

Data

This extension does not collect or store any personal data, browsing history, or any information that can personally identify you through YT-Tracker.

In the future, the Extension may collect non-personal information, such as browser version, platform name, display settings, and user filter settings. This data will be used solely for enhancing the Extension's functionality, including implementing new features, improving existing ones, and suggesting default settings for new users. No personally identifiable information or website-specific data will be collected or shared.

Third-Party Services

The Extension employs certain third-party services for specific functionalities:

  • Storage API: YT-Tracker uses browser-specific storage mechanisms (cookies) to store user settings and preferences securely. We do not transmit or share this data with any third parties.
  • Payment Processors: In the event of donations, in the future we may use third-party payment processors (e.g., Stripe, PayPal) to facilitate financial transactions securely. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for information on how your data is handled during these transactions.
